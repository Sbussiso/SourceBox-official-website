Welcome to SourceBox!


SourceBox is a machine learning platform for both developers and consumers alike. Use our demos to get the most out of LLM's beyond ChatGPT and similar models. Harness the power of frameworks such as langchain and platforms like huggingface for a uniqe LLM experience. Spin up chat bots quick and easy with WikiDoc for custom Q/A from multiple data sources of different types including databases. Automate parts of LLM development with tools like CodeDoc. Demos

Get started right away with SourceBox demos. Demos are downloadable and instantly executable micro applications hosted on the SourceBox platform to provide services, display, and discover use cases for LLM's. WikiDoc and CodeDoc are two examples of demos available on the SourceBox platform.

Services

Get the most out of local LLM's with SourceBox services. SourceBox services range from no code services to developer project starter kits

Pack-Man

Safely put your personal data to work. Pack-Man is a data package manager service for creating, storing, and managing "packs" of custom data to use dynamicaly on various services across the SourceBox platform! For example you can connect packs to Source-Lightning and WikiDoc for easy custom Q/A, downloadable models, and templates with your own data included. All pack data is encrypted and optionaly stored on the SourceBox platform. Packs are also directly downloadable so you can keep your data local and even more secure. You can have peace of mind knowing your data is yours. Once you build a package the data is then encrypted and optionaly stored in our database or downloaded. Each downloaded pack comes with a "key" to undo the encryption to use outside of SourceBox services. Note: you do not need to decrypt packs to use them with SourceBox services. You only need to decrypt packs for usage outside SourceBox.

SourcePacks

SourcePacks are standard data packages that can be used accross the SourceBox platform on any service that is connected to Pack-Man. Build your SourcePacks by uploading local files, code, scraping webpages, and connecting databases.

CodePacks

CodePacks are like SourcePacks but for code related data specifically. CodePacks can also be used across the sourceBox platform on most services connected to the package manager (Pack-Man). CodePacks can be used to customize lightning templates and RAG (retrieval augmented generation) related services for more custom templates and generative solutions.
